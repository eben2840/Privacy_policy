**PRIVACY POLICY — OSTOMY UNIVERSITY**
**Last Updated: December 2025**

Ostomy University (“we”, “us”, “our”) provides educational tools, community support, and health-tracking features for ostomy patients, caregivers, and healthcare professionals. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Ostomy University mobile application (“the App”).

By installing or using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

---

### 1. Information We Collect

**1.1 Personal Information You Provide**

* Name
* Email address (for username/password login or Google login)
* Optional profile details
* Care-related entries you manually log (ostomy changes, supplies, notes)
* Community interactions such as posts or comments (if available)

**1.2 Sensitive Health Information**
If you use tracking features, you may enter health-related data such as ostomy metrics, supply usage, routines, and notes.
This data is optional.
We do **not** sell, share, or use this information for advertising.

**1.3 Automatically Collected Data**

* Device type
* Operating system
* App usage statistics
* Crash logs
* Analytics information
* IP address (for security and fraud prevention)

**1.4 Google Sign-In Data**
If you sign in using Google, we receive only your Google account name and email.
We do not access contacts, files, or any other Google information.

---

### 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

* Provide and operate App features
* Save your logs, preferences, and tracking entries
* Enable secure login and account management
* Send reminders and notifications
* Support community features
* Improve the App through analytics and troubleshooting
* Respond to user support requests
* Protect the App from fraud or misuse

We do **not** sell your data.

---

### 3. Data Storage and Security

* Your data is stored on secure, encrypted servers.
* All sensitive information is transmitted via encrypted HTTPS connections.
* Access to server-side data is restricted to authorized system processes only.
* We apply industry-standard security measures to prevent unauthorized access, data loss, misuse, and breaches.

You are responsible for protecting your login credentials.

---

### 4. Sharing of Information

We do **not** sell or share your personal or health data for advertising or marketing.

We may share information only in the following cases:

**4.1 Service Providers**
We use trusted third parties for authentication, analytics, and cloud hosting. These providers process data only to support App operations and follow strict confidentiality rules.

**4.2 Legal Compliance**
We may disclose data if required by laws, regulations, or government requests.

**4.3 Protection and Security**
We may share limited information to prevent fraud, abuse, or threats to user safety.
